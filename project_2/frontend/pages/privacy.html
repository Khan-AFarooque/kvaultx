Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026 • KvaultX Zero-Knowledge Security

🔐 Your Privacy is Our Core Mission. At KvaultX, we believe your passwords, notes, and credentials belong strictly to you. We design our architecture so we NEVER sell, monetize, or read your private data.

1. Zero-Knowledge Encryption Guarantee

KvaultX is engineered from the ground up on a Zero-Knowledge Architecture. This means:

  • Your master password and vault items are encrypted client-side using industry-standard AES-256 GCM encryption before sending data to our servers.
  • Our servers store ONLY encrypted ciphertext blob data. We do NOT hold master encryption keys or unencrypted passwords.
  • Even if compelled by third parties or database breaches, unencrypted vault data cannot be read by anyone except YOU.

2. Information We Collect

We minimize data collection to the absolute bare necessities required to operate the service:

  • Account Credentials: Full Name and Email Address (used for authentication, welcome emails, security alerts, and OTP verification).
  • Master Password Hash: Cryptographically hashed using Bcrypt with 10 salt rounds (never plain-text).
  • Security Logs: Basic security metadata (IP address, login timestamp, browser user-agent) stored strictly for security alert notifications and active session tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to:

  • Authenticate your identity when logging in, changing credentials, or resetting passwords.
  • Send automated Welcome Emails upon new account signup.
  • Send automated Security Login Alerts and Master Password Change Notifications with instant account lock capabilities.
  • Send Verification OTP codes to your registered email address.
  • Protect your account against brute-force attacks, unauthorized credential changes, and intruder logins.

We NEVER sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information or email address with any third-party advertisers.

4. Cookies & Token Storage

KvaultX uses secure, short-lived JWT (JSON Web Tokens) and HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax Cookies to maintain your authenticated session. We do NOT use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

5. Security Alerts & Immediate Account Blocking Protocol

For maximum privacy and protection against unauthorized account takeovers:

  • Whenever a successful login or master password modification occurs, an automated security notification is sent to your registered email address.
  • If an unauthorized person accesses your account using your personal email address, each security email contains a direct "🚨 Report & Block Account Immediately" button.
  • Clicking this button instantly revokes all active device sessions across all browsers/devices and locks the account to prevent unauthorized intruders from accessing your vault data.

6. Data Retention & Account Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may edit, export, or permanently delete your stored vault items at any time through the KvaultX Dashboard. If you delete your account, all associated encrypted vault items and account metadata are permanently purged from our databases.

7. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect enhancements in security protocols. Significant updates will be communicated via email notifications to all registered users.

8. Contact Privacy Team
